Dyslexia Screening

Online or in person diagnostic screening and assessment to ascertain you or your child's specific learning difficulty dyslexia (SpLD).
We help our clients determine whether specialist intervention classes are necessary and help them understand the strengths and weaknesses of their profile.

Beginning with an initial Dyslexia Screening consultation consisting of a pre-diagnostic interview and information gathering, we will first determine whether an assessment for our client will be suitable and necessary. Following on from the results of this initial screening, if it is deemed beneficial to our clients, they may proceed with Dyslexia Assessment.

Consisting of a selection of assessment tests, accredited to the standards of the British Dyslexia Association and carried out to the specifications of the SASC, our Dyslexia Assessment will allow us to identify particular areas of focus and concern with our clients. These assessments can take place over one or more sessions as appropriate, allowing for participant comfort breaks and personal schedule..

A feedback consultation will follow where the participant will be furnished with an assessment report containing recommendations specific to the individual relevant to their own home, work and education environments. Intervention Classes

Specialist Teaching Intervention Classes tailored to the results of each indiviual's assessment process where there is an intervention need.
Specific targets set out with the leaner in relation to their literary goals at the outset to help them best handle their learning challenge.

We will provide structured lessons, which aim to support the learner in recognition and development of their own metacognitive processing ( the process of developing an understanding about how we think and learn). This is the key focus of this intervention.

Classes will enable the learner to implement learned strategies across a number of settings(home, education & employment and are offered in blocks, which allow the learner to pick specific targets and take time to develop relevant skills. A multi-sensory approach is incorporated as there is strong evidence of the its benefits for dyslexic learners, in particular the Kelly and Phillips approach.

Business Consultation

Helping you adapt your working environment to optimise engagement and accessability for your dyslexic employees.
We will scale our services to support your change efforts in implementing the right tools to help minimize or prevent disruption for your workforce's day-to-day operations.

Dyslexic employees encounter a number of struggles which go unnoticed. From environmental challenges like lighting and cluttered workspaces to overloading of information, there can be many obstacles which dyslexic employees face in their day-to day tasks.

We offer employers strategies to support their affected employees such as helping to increase visual supports in the work environment, adapting policies and procedures into accessible language and reviewing internal documentation which employees must complete and comprehend.

We can help your business in creating a more dyslexia friendly working environment so that all employees feel valued and help you foster a more productive and successful workplace.
